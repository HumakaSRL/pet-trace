Our Commitment to GDPR

The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) is a comprehensive privacy law enacted by the European Union to protect personal data and ensure transparency in how organizations collect, store, and process it.

Key Points of GDPR

  • Personal Data: GDPR defines personal data as any information that can identify an individual, including names, emails, IP addresses, and online identifiers.
  • Data Subject Rights: Individuals have several rights under the GDPR, including:
    • Right to Access: The right to know what personal data is being processed.
    • Right to Rectification: The right to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
    • Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten): The right to request deletion of personal data under certain conditions.
    • Right to Restrict Processing: The right to limit how personal data is processed.
    • Right to Data Portability: The right to transfer data to another service provider.
    • Right to Object: The right to object to certain processing activities.
  • Consent: Organizations must obtain clear, informed, and specific consent from individuals to collect and process their personal data. Consent must be easily withdrawable.
  • Data Protection by Design and Default: Organizations must integrate data protection into their operations from the outset and ensure that, by default, only the necessary data is processed.
  • Data Breach Notification: Organizations must notify relevant authorities and affected individuals within 72 hours of discovering a data breach.

Public Data Disclosure

By using Pet Trace, you acknowledge that all data you provide on the platform, including but not limited to pet owners' contact information (such as phone numbers and email addresses), will be publicly visible. This includes any information you enter related to the pets, such as microchip numbers, pet names, and owner contact details. There are no restrictions on the visibility of this data once it is submitted to the website.

We encourage you to only enter information that you are comfortable sharing publicly. The purpose of this transparency is to facilitate pet tracking and identification in case pets are lost.
