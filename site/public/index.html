How Pet Trace Works

Simple steps to ensure your pet's safety and quick reunion if they ever get lost

1

Easy Registration

Pet owners and professionals register pets in seconds with just the microchip ID and basic contact info.

2

Instant ID Lookup

Anyone who finds your pet can immediately access your secure contact details by searching the microchip ID.

3

Quick Reunion

The finder sees your contact information immediately and can reach out to you directly for a quick reunion.
